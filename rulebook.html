📜 OFFICIAL RULEBOOK

1. General Rules

  • All games must be played on the official league server.
  • Respect all players and staff at all times.
  • No exploiting, glitching, or unsportsmanlike conduct.
  • Matches must be recorded for review if needed.

2. Rosters & Transactions

  • Maximum roster size: 24 players
  • Trades must be approved by both teams and commissioner
  • Signings / Releases must be posted in #transactions
  • Contract rules and salary cap (if applicable)

3. Gameplay & Penalties

  • Standard NFL rules with league-specific modifications
  • Excessive celebrations or taunting = warning / fine
  • Disconnects during critical moments may result in forfeit
